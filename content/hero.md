# Poor Man's Platform

**Simplify platform creation for teams of any size**

PMP (Poor Man's Platform) is a comprehensive ecosystem of tools and applications designed to help you build and manage a complete platform infrastructure without the enterprise price tag. Whether you're a solo developer, a small team, or a growing organization, PMP provides the essential building blocks you need.
